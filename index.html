Project3

Solar Tracking System

Our Arduino-based Solar Tracking System is a smart and efficient solution designed to maximize the efficiency of solar panels. By using light sensors and servos, this innovative IoT project ensures that solar panels are always aligned with the sun's position throughout the day. The system continuously monitors the sunlight's angle and adjusts the orientation of the solar panels accordingly, ensuring they capture the maximum amount of sunlight. This not only optimizes energy production but also contributes to a more sustainable and eco-friendly energy source. With the Arduino Solar Tracking System, we're harnessing the power of IoT technology to improve renewable energy utilization, making solar energy an even more viable and sustainable option for a greener future.
